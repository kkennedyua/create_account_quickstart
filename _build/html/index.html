Create an Account and Add Services¶

Goal¶

This Quick Start walks you through the steps to creating a CyVerse account. Your CyVerse account gives you access to several different apps and services, including the Discovery Enviroment and the CyVerse Wiki, which contains how-to information, and subscribes you to that service’s email notifications about maintenance periods and outages, and to CyVerse’s newsletter, Node.

In addition, there are other CyVerse services, such as Atmosphere and BisQue, to which you may request access.

Register for an account¶

  1. To register for a CyVerse account, go to the CyVerse user management portal.
  2. Complete the information on the form.

Hint

Use an institutional email address if you intend to add access

to Atmosphere, CyVerse’s cloud-computing platform.

  1. Click the link in the email you receive to validate your email and begin using your account.

Add additional services¶

In addition to the default services that are provided to you when you sign up for a new account, you can request access to others. For more information on the list of all CyVerse apps and platforms, see the CyVerse Products web page.

  1. Go to the CyVerse user management portal, click Login at the top, and enter your CyVerse username and password.

  2. In the Available Services section at the bottom of the Apps & Services tab, find the app or service to add, and then click Request Access.

  3. Repeat for each service to add.

  4. If requesting access to Atmosphere, specify the instance size, including the number of CPUs, memory in GB, and root storage in GB, needed for your instance.

    Start small. Start with the smallest size until you know for certain which size you need. You can always size up but you can’t size down.

Note

If you did not use an institutional email address (as required

by Atmosphere), you will need to change your email address.

  1. When the service has been added, you will receive an email with access instructions and may begin using the service.
